What the light means

Green is not a palette. It is the indicator after the checks pass.

The reference is mission control: a green lamp on a control panel, not a brand mood board. Work gets the green light when it is useful, reviewed, reversible where it needs to be, and pointed at human good instead of novelty for its own sake.

Flight rules

Human mission. Results oriented. Operators accountable.

Our Mission: 1. Support our workers. 2. Make the world world incrementally for average citizens. 3. Collaborate on fun projects. (In that order.)

We like hard systems, but the point is not machinery. The point is better outcomes for real people: workers first, average citizens next, and fun projects after the important lights are stable.

We prefer evidence over theater, review over vibes, and useful control panels over decorative dashboards. When the light turns green, it should mean something.
