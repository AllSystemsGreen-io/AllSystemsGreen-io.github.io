Privacy

This public site is intentionally simple: no accounts, no forms, no cookies, no analytics, and no third-party embeds.

What We Collect

The site itself does not ask you to submit information and does not run analytics, advertising pixels, tracking cookies, chat widgets, or embedded third-party scripts.

If you email us, your email address and the contents of your message are processed by our email provider and by the people or tools we use to respond. Do not send secrets, credentials, sensitive personal data, or confidential information unless we have a written agreement for that exchange.

Technical Logs

Static hosting providers, domain registrars, DNS providers, browsers, and network operators may create routine logs such as IP address, user agent, timestamps, requested URL, and basic security events. We do not use this public site to build visitor profiles.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to email inquiries and potential collaboration conversations.
  • To maintain site availability, security, and abuse prevention.
  • To comply with law or protect rights, safety, and property when necessary.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information from this site. We may share information with service providers that help us operate email, hosting, security, legal, accounting, or business administration functions.

Location

AllSystemsGreen.io, LLC is associated with Montana / Silver Bow County. If you contact us from another jurisdiction, your message may be processed in the United States.
